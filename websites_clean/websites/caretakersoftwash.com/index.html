Welcome to Caretaker Softwash- Your Professional Myrtle Beach Pressure Washing Company

Our mission is simple. We strive to clean the Grand Strand one house, one business, one fence, one deck, and one roof at a time. When we come to your home or business, our goal is to improve the exterior aesthetic beauty, while helping you maintain the value of your property. Our “soft” pressure washing technique leaves surfaces clean without the damage that other harsh pressure washers leave behind. We offer a variety of cleaning services including residential and commercial pressure washing, deck cleaning and restoration, cleaning walkways and driveways, fence cleaning, gutter cleaning, roof cleaning, and even specialized tent cleaning. Call us today at (843) 602-0821 to schedule an appointment, and as always, we are happy to provide FREE estimates. We appreciate your business!
