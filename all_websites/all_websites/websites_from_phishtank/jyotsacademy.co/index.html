JYOT’S ADVANCED AESTHETICS ACADEMY

A Private College Under Ther Private Career Colleges Act, 2005

We are all about Education, Beauty, Health and Wellness
Jyot’s Advanced Aesthetics Academy is a vibrant and exciting educational institution that has been providing comprehensive education in cosmetology for over 15 years. Each graduate of Jyot’s Advanced Aesthetics Academy is presently realizing their dreams of a glamorous and fulfilling career in the beauty industry. With courses in Hairstyling and Advanced Aesthetics Diploma Programs, students are prepared for a fast-paced and ever-changing career in the Beauty Industry. With highly experienced Instructors and numerous opportunities to apply the skills acquired. Education at Jyot’s Advanced Aesthetics Academy is unparalleled!
