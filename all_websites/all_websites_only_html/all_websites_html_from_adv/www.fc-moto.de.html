Helstons Artic Ladies Motorcycle Textile Jacket

A simple but stylish ladies winter jacket with a fake fur hood and two outside pockets that offer enough storage space for your personal belongings.

Features:

  • solid lining (body / sleeves) with 2 zip pockets
  • adjustable cuffs and sides
  • 4 outside pockets
  • inside pockets 
  • removable protection (shoulders, elbows and back) homologated
  • protection against rain by a membrane
  • PrimaLoft® padded jacket

Additional product information

Material Outer: 98% nylon, 2% leather/ Lining: 59% cotton, 31% polyester/ Collar: 100% polyester
