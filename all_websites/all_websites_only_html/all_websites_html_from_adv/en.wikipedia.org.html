Europe Raiders (Chinese: 欧洲攻略) is a 2018 action thriller film directed by Jingle Ma, as the third film in his Raiders franchise, following Tokyo Raiders (2000) and Seoul Raiders (2006).[2] The film was released on August 17, 2018.[3]

Plot[edit]

Lam Choi Fung and Wang Chao Ying are known respectively as the number 1 and 2 of the bounty hunter world for many years. When a surveillance programme 'Hand of God‘ created by world's top hacker Mercury gets stolen, the CIA sends both Lam and Wang to investigate.

Cast[edit]

  • Tony Leung Chiu-wai as Mr. Lam
  • Kris Wu as Rocky
  • Tiffany Yan Tang as Ms. Wang
  • Du Juan as Sophie
  • George Lam
  • Jakob Graf as Peter Lawson
  • Alberto Lancellotti as an Italian mafia boss [4]
  • Daniel Gutin as CIA Intelligence Director [5]

Production[edit]

Filming began on August 31, 2016 in Italy.[6]

Reception[edit]

As of the opening weekend, the Europe Raiders film has received a score of 9/10 review from Avi Offer, who described it as a crowd pleasing thrill ride.[7]

The film debuted promisingly in second place on its opening Friday in China, but dropped off to the fifth and the seventh place on the weekend due to terrible word of mouth.[8]
