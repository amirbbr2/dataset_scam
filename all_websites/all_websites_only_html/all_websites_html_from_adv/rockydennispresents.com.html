Here’s How I Get Free Crypto

Website Traffic
 

BetFury

This. Right here.

Truth be told, as important as BetFury is in terms of maximizing your satoshis and generating passive income, it’ll be slow going unless we inject more satoshis. (Keno, autopick, low risk, 1 coin bets = highest ROI of all mining BFG strategies).

 

CCBOX.IO

I recommend setting this account up early on. Reason is, some faucets will “help” you set up a CCBOX and if you’re like me you’ll wind up with faucets that can’t deposit anywhere. At the time of this writing, changing your CCBOX key on some faucets isn’t working. Bonus, CCBOX has links to working faucets for a variety of currencies including BitCoin Gold!

 

CoinPayU

This site is very reliable, very responsive and easy to navigate. What it lacks is a faucet, and the method of converting coins though simple, is not immediately obvious or intuitive. I have, however, found that its Offerwall partners seem to overall be more reliable than a lot of others I’ve come across.

 

Cointiply

I feel the same about this one as Free-TRX. Actually, I think Cointiply is better, though I’m not sure why. This came highly recommended to me. I can’t say I’m disappointed, because I’m not, but I can’t remember if there was something particularly special going on. Especially since the faucet cooldown is 1 hour.

 

Topcoins.pw.

Topcoins.pw

This is a regular 15 minute timer, but a pretty quick confirmation process, like Crane-Five pretty much. I use this faucet for Tron, but they also have BAT, BCH, and BNB. I don’t use Binance so I’m unsure how valuable BNB is to a user, but hey, here’s a faucet you can use for it.

 

Althub

14 cryptocurrencies. BTC, ETH, and LTC are FaucetPay only. But everything else is Direct Withdraw. XLM, DGB, ADA, TRX, BTT, ZEC, DASH, RDD, SYS, ETN, PIVX, XVG. DOGE goes both ways. The site seems to be a bit newer, so there’s that. The only real problem I have? You need to get to level 7 before you can access the Offerwall.

CryptoTab

Free satoshis just for having the browser open. This means you can leave it on in the background. Direct withdraw to your wallet.

Speaking of wallets, BetFury’s Bitcoin and Tron wallets have no associated transfer fees. This is great especially starting out, you don’t need to be paying $7 just for sending/receiving $1 worth of bits.

 

ExpressCrypto

This is my microwallet of choice. Actually, the only real option. A lot of faucets deposit directly into your EC account, meaning you can consolidate all your various bits from multiple sources. Also, this works in the United States, FP doesn’t. You can exchange currencies here, as well as stake a few. (Staking means you park your currency with them, and you earn interest on it).

 

FireFaucet

This one is definitely standing out in its performance. All these sites are what they are, and how you farm one may not be how the next guy farms the other. That said, I pulled in over 100,000 satoshi in one day. from FireFaucet. Half of it went into 10% BetFury boxes (basically staking BTC, the other half went into mining BFG. 100sat bets on Keno, medium risk, autopick minus 2 picks).

 

CoinAdster

This faucet has a 5 minute cooldown. That alone makes this well worth signing up to. The only weird thing is, on the login you can check a box that says, “Keep me signed in for a week.”. It doesn’t do anything. Or it does and my browser settings interfere with it somehow. Who cares, at 5 minutes a pop, that’s still 3x more than the leading brand.

 

TronWarz

A solid site. I think the banner hypes it up a bit more than necessary, but YMMV. But hey. free reliable crypto bits is what we’re after, and TronWarz deliverz. I’ll take a reliable, responsive, guaranteed to work source of average amounts of coin over sites that promise everything and deliver nothing.

 

FaucetCrypto

This is a really solid site faucet site with minimal BS. You won’t have to collect faucets and click shortlinks for 3 weeks just to withdraw a few satoshis. Also, the coin conversion ratio is particularly good for Tron. This makes getting a few pieces of crypto just to play with a lot more feasible and a lot more cost effective.

 

PipeFlare

Fast, responsive site that doesn’t bombard you with popups and other annoying crap. Direct withdrawal of DOGE, ZEC, and FLR. I have no idea if Flare tokens are worth going after, ZEC probably? I just like the DOGE directly to my wallet. Not for any particularly practical purpose, I just like to accumulate DOGE.

 

Free-TRX

I was ridiculously stoked when I first found this site, because it was leaps and bounds better than the other junk I was running into. My opinion has gone down a bit on it, as a result of finding better jams. Still worth it to get that faucet. Job checking doesn’t seem to be happening right now, but maybe it’s just the time of the year.

 

CraneFive.

Crane-Five

This faucet is a 10 minute cooldown, and has a partner site giving out coupons for it daily. But that’s not even the best part…The collecting process is the fastest I’ve found so far. So fast. This won’t make you wealthy, but it’ll generate enough BTG fast enough to be able to transfer to your wallet. They got EOS, BSV, ZEN, SIN and of course BTG.

LarvelFaucet

LarvelFaucet offers withdrawals to both FaucetPay and ExpressCrypto, as well as Direct Wallet. Solid. I really like the faucet here. The logical part of my brain says that there’s nothing special going on here, but the less practical side really wants to believe. Either way, it doesn’t do any harm to stop in once an hour. Plus you get an Activity Point for collecting, which will come in handy if you start spedning more time on the site.
